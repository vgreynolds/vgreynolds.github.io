Outside of Work

About 20 years ago, I taught myself to quilt using tv shows and instruction books. My first quilt was ... functional. In fact, I still have it and it has held up well. Once I took an in-person course, however, my skills improved dramatically because I learned to be more attentive to details, such as measuring accurately!
Over time, my style has also changed from traditional patterns, to playing with abstract and modern designs and fabrics.
